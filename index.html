The person behind the code

Addis Ababa · 2025

A software engineering graduate building toward international, product-driven engineering — equal parts pragmatic builder and curious analyst.

I hold a Bachelor's in Software Engineering and am currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Analytics. My day-to-day is split between shipping cross-platform mobile apps in Flutter and growing a foundation in Python, exploratory data analysis, and visualization.

I've shipped real software in the field — building a maintenance-tracking and ticketing system as a mobile developer intern at the Ethiopian Space Science & Geospatial Institute, after two and a half years as a technical consultant.
